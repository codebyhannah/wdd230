Boise Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Overview

Site Name

Boise Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Boise Chamber of Commerce promotes business growth and economic development through business advocacy, networking and leadership opportunities, promoting individual businesses, and events for the whole community. Our mission is to serve and expand our business community and facilitate public and private partnerships to build a vibrant economy, a prosperous community, and maintain the region's quality of life.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?
  • What networking opportunities are available through the chamber?
  • How does the chamber contribute to the community?
  • How does the chamber contribute to its member businesses?
